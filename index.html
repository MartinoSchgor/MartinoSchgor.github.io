Wear Headphones!
(Earbuds are fine too)
You will hear an audio signal. The button "ON/OFF" applies an effect that distorces the sound, and the slider below regulates the intensity of the distortion.
If the slider is set full left, the distortion is absent and the button will not modify the sound at all.
If the slider is set full right, the distortion is maximized.
Please, adjust the slider to the point where the button starts having any audible effect, then confirm by clicking NEXT.
The test is infinite, when you are fed up, click on QUIT.
